Founder & Consultant Psychologist

Amelia
Simpson

Consultant Psychologist  ·  CDA Licensed, UAE  ·  HCPC Registered, UK

"I built Psynara because I know what it means to find something that genuinely changes you. That is what I want to offer to others."

Amelia Simpson is a Consultant Psychologist and the founder of Psynara, holding clinical licences in both the UAE and the United Kingdom. Her work brings together the analytical precision of evidence-based psychology with the compassionate presence of someone who has experienced the transformative power of immersive retreat work herself.

Amelia's journey into integrative healing began not in theory but through lived experience. That experience led her to bring together clinical excellence with somatic and holistic practice, and to create Psynara as a space where others could access the same depth of transformation. She does not offer programmes. She offers a relationship, and a process built entirely around the person in front of her.

CDA Licensed, UAE HCPC Registered, UK Schema Therapy EMDR EFT Gottman Method Discernment Therapy Somatic Practice

Consultant Psychologist

Dr Jamie
Stephenson

DCounsPsych  ·  HCPC Registered  ·  CDA Licensed, UAE  ·  Chartered Member, BPS

Dr Jamie Stephenson is a Consultant Psychologist with a doctorate in Counselling Psychology and a clinical career spanning NHS specialist services, private practice, corporate wellness, and postgraduate teaching. He brings a rare breadth of experience and a deep commitment to the therapeutic relationship as the foundation of all meaningful change.

His approach is warm, collaborative, and integrative, drawing on cognitive behavioural, psychodynamic, and person-centred frameworks alongside specialist training in EMDR and Schema Therapy. At Psynara, Jamie leads retreats focused on trauma, emotional healing, addiction recovery, and complex relational difficulties.

Trauma & PTSD Anxiety Depression Addiction Recovery Grief & Loss Relational Difficulties Burnout

Consultant Psychotherapist

Eugene
Traboulsi

Consultant Clinical Social Worker  ·  CDA Licensed, UAE  ·  AASW Registered

Eugene Traboulsi is a Consultant Psychotherapist and Clinical Social Worker with over two decades of experience across complex mental health presentations. Known for his natural intuition, authenticity, and ability to meet people with both warmth and directness, he creates a therapeutic environment where genuine openness becomes possible.

Working within an integrative framework of CBT, Solution Focused Therapy, and Person-Centred approaches, Eugene ensures that every client is an active participant in their own process. At Psynara, he brings particular depth to retreats focused on burnout, relational healing, addiction, and identity.
