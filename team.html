Who We Are

Chosen not only for their expertise,
but for their ability to create the safety that real transformation requires.

Psynara clinicians are fully qualified, licensed, and deeply experienced practitioners who have worked across a wide range of clinical settings. They bring evidence-based training and genuine compassion to every retreat they lead. At Psynara, they do not deliver programmes. They build relationships, and craft experiences tailored entirely around the person in front of them.

Jamie

Consultant Psychologist

Dr Jamie
Stephenson

DCounsPsych  ·  HCPC Registered  ·  CDA Licensed, UAE  ·  Chartered Member, BPS

"Creating a safe, non-judgmental space is not just the starting point. It is the foundation on which everything else is built."

Dr Jamie Stephenson is a Consultant Psychologist with a doctorate in Counselling Psychology and a clinical career spanning NHS specialist services, private practice, corporate wellness, and university teaching at masters and doctorate level. He brings to each retreat a rare breadth of experience across therapeutic modalities, life stages, and presenting difficulties.

His approach is warm, collaborative, and deeply human. He believes the therapeutic relationship is not simply a vehicle for treatment, it is treatment in itself. Working within an integrative framework, he draws on cognitive behavioural, psychodynamic, and person-centred approaches, alongside specialist training in EMDR and Schema Therapy, to meet each client exactly where they are.

At Psynara, Jamie leads retreats focused on trauma, emotional healing, addiction recovery, and complex relational difficulties, bringing both clinical precision and genuine compassion to every day of the process.

Eugene

Consultant Psychotherapist

Eugene
Traboulsi

Consultant Clinical Social Worker  ·  CDA Licensed, UAE  ·  AASW Registered

"Healing happens in relationship. My role is to create the conditions where that becomes possible."

Eugene Traboulsi is a Consultant Psychotherapist and Clinical Social Worker with over two decades of experience working with individuals and families across some of the most complex presentations in mental health. His career has spanned government and non-government clinical roles internationally, with deep expertise in dual diagnosis, trauma, relational difficulties, and life transitions.

Known for his natural intuition, authenticity, and ability to meet people with both warmth and directness, Eugene creates a therapeutic environment where genuine openness becomes possible. He works within an integrative framework drawing on CBT, Solution Focused Therapy, and Person-Centred approaches, always ensuring that the client is an active participant in shaping the process rather than a passive recipient of it.

At Psynara, Eugene brings particular depth to retreats focused on burnout, relational healing, addiction, anxiety, and identity. His experience with expat adjustment and cross-cultural contexts makes him especially well-placed to support clients navigating life in the UAE and beyond.
